Our property slopes downhill from northwest to southeast, dropping about 3 meters across the site. Instead of fighting the slope, we'll work with it — creating terraced lawn platforms stepping down the hill, each separated by stone retaining walls. A 10-meter asphalt driveway connects the street to the property, leading to parking for 6 cars (side by side). In the middle: green lawn terraces. At the bottom: a swimming pool and outdoor kitchen (konoba). The existing concrete wall along the boundary will be covered with climbing plants, and a live evergreen hedge will provide privacy from the neighboring parcel.
